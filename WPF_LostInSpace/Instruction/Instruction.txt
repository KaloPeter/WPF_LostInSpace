Movement: Left & Right arrow keys
Shoot: space button
About the game: 
Traver as far as you can. Asteroids/Satellites make your travel more challenging.
You can shoot them, but be aware, if you shoot too much lasers, your pistol need cooldown.
You can get crystals, which later can be used to purchase new space suits
which offers different benefits.
You can registrate/modify/delete your user anytime.
